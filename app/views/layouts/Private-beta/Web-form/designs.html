High level research objectives

  1. Appetite for web form concepts: – how open are users to using the webform concepts as an alternative to API and spreadsheet approaches?
  2. Concept comprehension: – do users understand the web form concepts and how it fits into their current workflow?
  3. Alignment with user needs and workflows: – how well do the concepts support their waste management needs and workflows?
  4. Adoption signals: – what would support or prevent adoption of the web form approach in real life?
  5. Pain points and blockers: – are there any challenges users anticipate in using these concepts?
  6. Opportunities for improvement: – what can be improved about the concepts tested, so that it's an effective alternative to API and current spreadsheet waste tracking?

High level research questions

Understanding and Appeal

  1. how do users perceive the idea of completing waste movements through a web form instead of their current methods?

Comprehension and Usability

  1. how easily do users understand and navigate the concepts presented?
  2. what helps or hinders their ability to complete tasks confidently and efficiently?

Fit with Existing Workflows

  1. how well do the web form concepts align with users' current ways of working and their waste management processes?

Perceived Value and Efficiency

  1. what aspects of the web form experience make data entry feel easier, faster, or more manageable compared to existing tools?

Adoption and Trust

  1. what factors would encourage or discourage users from adopting the web form approach in real use?

Pain Points and Improvement Opportunities

  1. where do users experience confusion, friction, or unmet needs when interacting with the concepts?
  2. what opportunities exist to improve usability, comprehension, and confidence?

Behavioural and Workflow Insights

  1. in what circumstances might users need to change how they submit waste movements, or revisit previous records?
  2. what patterns or behaviours could shape how they use a future web form service?
