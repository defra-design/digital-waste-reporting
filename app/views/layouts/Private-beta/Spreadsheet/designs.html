User group: Waste receivers

October 2025 – March 2026

Spreadsheet

Tested Version 3A

Two tabs (waste movement and waste items, linked by a unique reference).

Version 3B

Single tab with nested structure for multiple waste items under one waste movement.

What we tested

This study focused on using the spreadsheet as a tool, to identify and understand:

  1. If participants can use the spreadsheets to enter waste movement and waste item data.
  2. Areas of the greatest burden to users.
  3. Areas of greatest data accuracy risks.

Spreadsheet

Tested Version 2 Errors

What we tested

  1. To understand if the spreadsheet solution is useable and useful.
  2. To understand if the spreadsheet option for non-API users is a viable solution as a concept.
  3. To understand if a user can successfully engage with the spreadsheet and points of friction, for example do they understand the cognitive model and ask of the spreadsheet.
  4. To get a better understanding of how applicable the spreadsheet solution is to the wider waste market.
