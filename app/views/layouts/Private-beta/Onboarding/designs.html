Onboarding / Registration

Tested Version 3

High level research objectives

  1. Registration – to understand if waste receivers can register without support when they join the new digital waste tracking service, especially their understanding of if they need to register each legal entity separately, and how they would expect to register these multiple legal entities.
  2. Onboarding to private beta – to evaluate waste receivers' understanding of the guidance pages, and what additional guidance is required by waste receivers, to support them to join the private beta service.
  3. Service charge – understand the most suitable method for paying the service charge, and test the full onboarding process for getting set up to use the service both in private beta and beyond.

High level research questions

  1. What does the registration process look like? Can we incorporate learnings from previous alpha work and the existing Defra one login?
  2. How do users feel about the registration journey we have designed, and do they work through this under testing?
  3. How can users pay the service charge? Is it the same person who registers that will also be able to make the payment?
  4. Can users understand and interpret the guidance pages in order to support themselves with onboarding, registration and any common technical issues?
  5. What information do users need to get onboarding to the service for both API and spreadsheet solutions?

High level research questions

  1. What does the registration process look like? Can we incorporate learnings from previous alpha work and the existing Defra one login?

  4. Can users understand and interpret the guidance pages in order to support themselves with onboarding, registration and any common technical issues?
  5. Can users add multiple legal entities to their Defra account, understand the relationships between them, and how they are managed separately?
