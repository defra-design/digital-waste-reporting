Onboarding / Registration

Tested Version 3

What we tested

  1. Registration – to understand if waste receivers can register without support when they join the new digital waste tracking service, especially their understanding of if they need to register each legal entity separately, and how they would expect to register these multiple legal entities.
  2. Onboarding to private beta – o evaluate waste receivers' understanding of the guidance pages, and what additional guidance is required by waste receivers, to support them to join the private beta service.
  3. Service charge – understand the most suitable method for paying the service charge, and test the full onboarding process for getting set up to use the service both in private beta and beyond.

Onboarding / Registration

Tested Version 2

What we tested

  1. Registration – to understand if waste receivers can register without support when they join the new digital waste tracking service, especially their understanding of if they need to register each legal entity separately, and how they would expect to register these multiple legal entities.
