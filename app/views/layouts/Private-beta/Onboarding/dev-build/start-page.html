Report receipt of waste

If you operate a licensed site that receives waste, you can use this service to:

  • register for a Defra account
  • dd businesses or organisations to your account
  • pay the annual service charge
  • get an API code to send us your waste movements

Before you start

You must check if your business or organisation's software can use our APIs. Contact your software provider if you are unsure.

You will also need to:

  • Sign in or create a Government Gateway or OneLogin account for your business or organisation.
  • Use your business or organisation's Government Gateway or OneLogin ID to register for a Defra account.
  • Provide the address, contact details and company registration number (if it is a limited company) of your business or organisation.
! Warning Do not use a personal Government Gateway account for this service.
