Why you need to register to report receipt of waste

From October 2026, all waste receiving sites that require a permit or license must record every load of waste they receive to be compliant with existing duty of care regulations.

As a receiver of controlled waste, you're responsible for ensuring accurate tracking of that waste. Being registered with the Receipt of Waste service allows you setup your sites for reporting waste receipts via your existing Waste Management software (or via an Excel based alternative).

Once in the service you will be able to:

  • Pay your service charge
  • Set up your sites
  • Access API Credentials
