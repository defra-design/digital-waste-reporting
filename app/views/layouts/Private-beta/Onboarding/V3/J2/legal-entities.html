What is a legal entity?

Examples of legal entities include:

  • a sole trader
  • a company
  • a partnership
  • a limited liability partnership (LLP)
  • large businesses

In large businesses made up of multiple companies or other bodies, each individual limited company or body that has control over a permitted waste operation that receives controlled waste must pay the fee individually.

If you have multiple legal entities, you can go back and add them to your business or organisation.
