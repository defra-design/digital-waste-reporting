Report receipt of waste

Sign into your Report receipt of waste account to If you receive waste via a permitted or licensed waste receiving site in the UK, sign into your account to create and manage your site API keys.

You should use this service if you're a permitted or licensed waste receiving site in the UK.

You can use this service to:

  • list your receiving sites and their associated addresses
  • generate your site API keys to be used in your waste management software
  • gain access to the alternative report receipt of waste method
  • sign your Receipt of waste declarations
  • pay your Report receipt of waste service charge

Before you start

Before you can use this service, you must:

  • Sign in or create a government gateway or OneLogin account for your business or organisation if you do not have one yet. Do not use a Government Gateway account created to access your personal information.
  • Sign in or register for a business or organisation Defra account using this Government Gateway or OneLogin ID if you do not have one yet

Because you're registering on behalf of a business or organisation, you'll also need:

  • the main contact details for your business or organisation (for example, head office contact details)
  • the Companies House number if the business or organisation is a limited company
The first account that you create for your business or organisation will automatically be an 'admin account'. This account can be used to create and approve additional admin or standard accounts within the same business or organisation.

The cost is £20 per year per individual legal entity, and gives the company the ability to report waste movements to DEFRA.

You'll need a debit or credit card to make your payment online.

Get help with a technical issue

You can contact the service support team if you need help with a technical issue, such as problems accessing the service.

Email: wastetracking@defra.gov.uk

Telephone 0800 876 6458

The service support team will not be able to answer regulatory questions.
