What we tested

Primary objectives

  1. Evaluate the usability, navigation and comprehension – of the “Send a waste movement” journey within the waste receiver account on the DWT service.
  2. Evaluate waste receivers' comprehension of DWT guidance – including whether it clearly sets expectations about:
  1. what DWT is?
  2. what WRs are required to do?
  3. how they should prepare to comply?

Specifically, this research will explore:

Whether WRs can:

  1. understand the guidance
  2. navigate to and choose an appropriate method for sending a waste movement
  3. locate and understand how to obtain an API code for automatic submission of waste movements
  4. send a waste movement using the spreadsheet approach

Key pain points, uncertainties and blockers across:

  1. the guidance and content
  2. the waste receiver account journeys

Opportunities to improve:

  1. the clarity and usefulness of Waste Receiver guidance
  2. the design of the waste receiver account and “send a waste movement” journeys
